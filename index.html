Questions & Answers

Does ReplyMind read all my emails?
No. ReplyMind only reads the specific email you are actively replying to, and only when you click Generate. It never runs in the background or scans your inbox.
Is my email content stored anywhere?
No. Your email text is sent to OpenAI to generate the reply, then immediately discarded. We never store, log, or sell your email content. Ever.
How do I activate Pro after paying?
After payment, email your PayPal receipt to support@tigerbrandsglobal.com with the subject "Pro Activation". We'll upgrade your account within 1 hour and send you confirmation.
How do I cancel?
Log into your PayPal account → Payments → Manage automatic payments → ReplyMind Pro → Cancel. Takes 30 seconds. No questions asked.
Does it work with Google Workspace accounts?
Yes. ReplyMind works with any Gmail account — personal @gmail.com and Google Workspace business accounts.
What language does it work in?
ReplyMind detects the language of the incoming email and replies in the same language. Works with Hebrew, English, Spanish, French, German, and most major languages.
