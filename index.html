Frequently Asked Questions

Does ReplyMind read all my emails?
No. ReplyMind only reads the specific email you are actively replying to, and only when you click Generate. It never scans your inbox or runs in the background.
Is my email content stored anywhere?
No. Your email text is sent to our secure backend and then to OpenAI to generate the reply, then immediately discarded. We never store, log, or sell your email content.
How is this different from AI chatbots?
AI chatbots are general-purpose. ReplyMind lives inside Gmail, knows your tone, detects email context, and inserts replies with one click. No tabs, no prompts, no copy-pasting.
What are "intents"?
Instead of getting random replies, you choose your intent: Accept, Decline, Maybe, Schedule, Delegate, More Info, Check In, Negotiate, Thank You, Apologize, Introduce, or Custom. ReplyMind generates one perfect reply tailored to that intent.
How do I activate after paying?
After payment, you'll receive an activation code. Enter it in the ReplyMind extension popup under "Activation Code". Your account upgrades instantly.
How do I cancel?
PayPal > Settings > Payments > Manage automatic payments > ReplyMind > Cancel. 30 seconds. No questions asked.
Does it work with Google Workspace?
Yes. Any Gmail account -- personal @gmail.com and Google Workspace business accounts.
What languages does it support?
ReplyMind auto-detects the incoming language and replies in the same one. English, Hebrew, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Portuguese, and more.
What is the "tone profile"?
A short description of how you communicate -- e.g., "Professional but warm, slightly casual, uses first names." Every reply is calibrated to match your style.
Is there a free trial?
The Free plan gives you 10 replies with no account, no credit card, and no expiration. Install and start immediately.
