Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Chrome Web Store Limited Use Statement: The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

What we collect

ReplyMind collects minimal data to provide its service:

  • Email address — when you create an account, used only for authentication and account management
  • Email message text — the text of the specific email you are actively replying to, collected only when you click "Generate Reply". This is transmitted to our server and to OpenAI for processing, then immediately discarded. It is never stored.
  • Contact notes — notes you voluntarily save about email contacts. Stored locally in your browser only, never on our servers.
  • Usage count — a count of how many replies you have generated, stored locally in your browser to manage the free tier limit.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not scan, read, or store your inbox or any emails other than the one you are actively replying to
  • We do not collect browsing history or activity on any website
  • We do not collect financial information
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not run any background processes when you are not using the extension

How we use your data

Email message text is used solely to generate reply suggestions via OpenAI's API. This data is transmitted securely via HTTPS, used for the single purpose of generating your reply, and then discarded. It is never stored on our servers, never sold, and never used for advertising or any other purpose.

Your email address is used solely to authenticate your account and send account-related communications (such as activation confirmation). We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

Third parties

We share email message text with OpenAI solely for the purpose of generating reply suggestions. OpenAI processes this data under their own privacy policy at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.

We use PayPal for payment processing. We never see or store your payment details. PayPal's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.

We do not sell, transfer, or share your data with any other third parties, including advertising platforms or data brokers.

Data security

All data transmission between the extension and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS. We do not log email content. Account passwords are stored as one-way hashed values and are never readable by us.

Your rights

You can delete your account at any time by emailing help@yourdomain.com. Upon deletion, all account data including your email address is permanently removed from our systems within 7 days.

Children

ReplyMind is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by email. Continued use of ReplyMind after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
