Common questions

How do I activate my Pro account after paying?
Email support@tigerbrandsglobal.com with your PayPal receipt attached and the subject line "Pro Activation". We'll upgrade your account and send confirmation within 1 hour during business hours.
The ⚡ button isn't appearing in Gmail
Try these steps: 1) Go to chrome://extensions and click the refresh icon on ReplyMind. 2) Refresh your Gmail tab (F5). 3) Open an email and click the Reply button — the Generate Reply button appears in the reply toolbar. If the toolbar injection fails, a floating ⚡ ReplyMind button appears at the bottom-right of the page instead.
Clicking the reply card doesn't insert into Gmail
Make sure you click the Reply button in Gmail first to open the compose box, then click Generate Reply. The insert feature requires an open compose box to work. If it still doesn't insert, it will copy to your clipboard as a fallback — just paste manually.
I'm getting a "Connection failed" error
This means the extension can't reach the backend server. Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, email us and we'll check the server status.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Log into PayPal → click your profile → Account Settings → Payments → Manage automatic payments → find ReplyMind Pro → click Cancel. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.
How do I set my tone profile?
Click the ⚡ ReplyMind icon in your Chrome toolbar → sign in to your account → you'll see a "Your Communication Style" text area. Write 1-3 sentences describing how you like to write emails (e.g. "Direct and professional, I keep emails short and always end with a clear next step."). Click Save. ReplyMind will use this for all future replies.
How do I delete my account?
Email support@tigerbrandsglobal.com with the subject "Delete my account". We'll remove all your data within 7 days and confirm by email.
