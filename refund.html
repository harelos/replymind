2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 Initial Subscription — 14-Day Full Refund

You may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase date. This applies to all paid plans (Basic, Pro, and Premium), whether billed monthly or annually. No reason is required.

If you are a consumer residing in the EU, EEA, or United Kingdom, you have a statutory right to withdraw from your purchase within 14 days of completing your transaction, in accordance with applicable consumer protection law and Paddle's Buyer Terms. 2.3 Subscription Renewals

If you did not intend to renew, or were charged in error, please contact us within 14 days of the renewal charge and we will issue a full refund for that renewal payment.

2.4 Free Plan

The Free plan requires no payment and therefore no refund applies.

3. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us at hello@replymind.xyz with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your ReplyMind account and your payment receipt or transaction ID (found in your Paddle purchase confirmation email)
  3. We will respond within 1–2 business days to confirm and process your refund via Paddle

4. Refund Processing

Once a refund is approved, Paddle will issue the refund to your original payment method. Processing time is typically 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer. Your subscription will be cancelled and your account downgraded to the Free plan. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed.

5. Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancellation stops future billing. You retain access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period and are not charged again. No refund is issued for the remaining days of the current period unless you request one within the 14-day refund window.

Refund returns the payment for the current period. Your subscription is cancelled immediately and your account is downgraded to the Free plan. To cancel without a refund, visit the Paddle billing portal linked in your subscription confirmation email, or contact us at hello@replymind.xyz.
