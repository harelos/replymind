7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Try any ReplyMind paid plan risk-free. If it's not for you, get a full refund — no questions asked.

1. Our Commitment

At ReplyMind, we want you to be completely satisfied with your subscription. We understand that a product may not always meet your expectations, which is why we offer a straightforward refund policy for all paid subscriptions.

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 Initial Subscription — 7-Day Full Refund

If you are not satisfied with your ReplyMind subscription, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase date. This applies to all paid plans (Basic, Pro, and Premium), whether billed monthly or annually.

  • The refund request must be submitted within 7 calendar days of your first payment
  • No reason is required — simply contact us and we will process your refund
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method

2.2 Renewal Payments

For subscription renewals (beyond the initial 7-day period), refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. We will consider refund requests for renewals in the following circumstances:

  • You were charged after you believed you had cancelled your subscription
  • You experienced a significant service outage or technical issue that prevented you from using ReplyMind during the billing period
  • A duplicate or erroneous charge occurred

For renewal refund requests, please contact us within 14 days of the renewal charge.

2.3 Annual Plans

For annual subscriptions:

  • The 7-day full refund guarantee applies from the date of the annual payment
  • After the initial 7-day window, pro-rata refunds may be considered for unused months if requested within 30 days of the annual payment, at our discretion
  • If you wish to switch from annual to monthly billing, contact us and we will assist with the transition

2.4 Free Plan

The Free plan requires no payment and therefore no refund applies. The Free plan provides 10 lifetime replies with no credit card required.

3. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will generally not be issued in the following cases:

  • Requests made after the applicable refund window has passed (7 days for initial, 14 days for renewals)
  • Accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Service (including abuse, spam, or fraudulent activity)
  • Dissatisfaction with AI-generated reply quality that is inherent to AI technology (e.g., occasional inaccuracies) — though we welcome feedback to improve the service
  • Failure to use the Service does not automatically qualify for a refund, though we will consider such requests on a case-by-case basis

4. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us at hello@replymind.xyz with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your ReplyMind account and your payment receipt or transaction ID
  3. We will respond within 1–2 business days to confirm your refund
  4. Refund processing typically takes 5–10 business days to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider

5. Refund Processing

Once a refund is approved:

  • The refund is issued to your original payment method (credit card, debit card, or other payment method used for the purchase)
  • Processing time is typically 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider
  • Your subscription will be cancelled and your account will be downgraded to the Free plan
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed

6. Cancellation vs. Refund

Please note the difference between cancellation and refund:

  • Cancellation: Stops future billing. You retain access to paid features until the end of your current billing period. No refund is issued for the current period.
  • Refund: Returns payment for the current period. Access to paid features is revoked immediately and your account is downgraded to the Free plan.

If you simply want to stop being charged going forward but keep access through the end of your billing period, cancellation is the right option. See our Terms of Service for cancellation instructions.

7. Chargebacks

If you have a billing issue, please contact us at hello@replymind.xyz before initiating a chargeback with your payment provider. We are committed to resolving any billing concerns quickly and fairly. Filing a chargeback without first contacting us may result in account suspension.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated via our website or by email. Continued use of ReplyMind after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
